\(512\times 512\) random double precision with sorting

The input matrix is a random d512.A.

The final image before convergence is actually the state at the end of the previous (i.e., last) cycle.
The other images show the state at the beginning of cycles.

For each element \(a_{ij}\ne 0\) the corresponding pixel is colored according to \(\lg|a_{ij}|\), while the zeros are colored separately.

For each strategy the images share the colormap, but it varies slightly between strategies.
The purpose of this example is to give insight into the structure of the iteration matrix, and not to show its actual elements.
